# Conquest Fire Temple Guide — Bring Burn Cures!

*Original: https://www.inven.co.kr/board/aion2/6444/655*

---

## Prep

Monsters use burn attacks. Buy **Burn Cure Potions** from general merchants — cheaper and more efficient than spamming HP potions.

---

## Route

Split tasks instead of sticking together. Rush center → climb behind mobs. One player: right Obelisk. Two players: left and right **Coolant Control**. Without control, floor damage persists. Skip coolant for speed in some parties.

**Rotan** (patrol boss): Avoid — no cube reward. If aggroed, hide at first boss entrance ledge; aggro resets. Use Tab (PC) to see aggro ranges.

---

## Mid Boss: Black Smoke Murt

Arms up = floor pattern. Dodge. Foot stomp = growing floor; back off or just-dodge into melee for speed.

---

## Mid Boss: Crimson Flame Ignus

**Don’t stack.** Arms up = floor under each player. **"Poop" pattern:** Green HP bar → 3 sec → permanent floor under you. Run back and drop it where others won’t step.

---

## Final Boss: Chromede's Desire

- **Basic attacks:** Wide cone — stay behind boss.
- **Clone attack:** Boss moves center → clone per player, 2 hits each. Spread out.
- **Bow down:** Bow aimed at floor → massive fire burst. Back off or use invuln.
- **Fire wall:** Sidestep advancing walls. At low HP, adds projectiles. After pattern, attack diamond landing spot for extra DPS.
