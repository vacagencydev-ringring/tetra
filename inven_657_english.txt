## Guide scope
- This guide is aimed at Cleric players who want a stable hybrid healing/damage setup.
- It is especially useful for players still organizing their progression priorities.
- Focus: practical investment order, healing reliability, and meaningful DPS contribution.

---

## Skill system fundamentals

- Specialization slots unlock at key level breakpoints.
- Core active skills should reach meaningful breakpoints early.
- Additional skill levels come from board nodes, arcana, and gear-based skill options.

Practical implication:
- Build planning should include both raw level investment and external level boosts.

---

## Damage skill direction

## Earth Punishment line
- Recommended to secure at least medium investment early.
- Supports baseline DPS consistency.

## Lightning Judgment line
- Important breakpoint appears at higher level due to additional trigger value.
- Good for pressure in burst windows.

## Conviction line
- Very high practical value after key breakpoint.
- Mobility conversion and reset chance make it strong in real mechanics.
- In current context, Conviction-heavy routing can outperform older alternatives.

---

## Healing skill direction

## Regeneration Light
- Keep at functional breakpoint for sustain and defensive stability.

## Healing Light
- Cooldown reduction and max-HP scaling effects are key.
- Essential in repeated damage scenarios.

## Recovery Radiance
- One of the most important healing investments.
- Charged usage provides major emergency recovery value.

Transition note:
- Temporary stigma choices are acceptable before your key healing breakpoints are complete.
- Replace temporary options as core healing levels are secured.

---

## Passive and utility logic

- Cleric performance is not only about raw heals.
- Defensive passives and utility uptime determine success in high-pressure fights.
- Build passives to support cast stability and sustain under interruption risk.

---

## Debanion and Arcana planning

- Plan debanion node path around your most-used active skills.
- Arcana should support your role split:
- More survival if you are the primary safety anchor.
- More offensive support if your party already has high sustain.

---

## Role execution in party play

- Cleric should not panic-heal every small hit.
- Use controlled cadence:
- Preventive sustain,
- Burst recovery on real danger windows,
- DPS contribution during safe intervals.

This cadence is what separates stable clerics from overheal-heavy unstable play.

---

## Common mistakes

- Over-investing in low-impact skills before healing core breakpoints.
- Ignoring cooldown reduction effects on major heals.
- Playing fully reactive instead of proactive timing.
- Neglecting movement/cast safety in pattern-heavy encounters.

---

## Practical checklist

- [ ] Do I have core healing skills at practical breakpoints?
- [ ] Is my damage setup aligned with my healing responsibility?
- [ ] Are temporary stigma choices still needed or outdated?
- [ ] Is my cast timing planned around boss mechanics?
- [ ] Am I contributing safe DPS in downtime windows?

---

## Practical conclusion
A strong Cleric build is a balance of healing reliability and controlled offensive contribution. Prioritize key breakpoints, maintain cooldown discipline, and adjust arcana/stigma to your real party role instead of static theory.
