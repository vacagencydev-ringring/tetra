# Transcendence Stages 1–10: Quick Guide

*Original: https://www.inven.co.kr/board/aion2/6444/695*

---

## Deus Research Base — Route

[1st named Gelkos → 2nd named Nazmun → mob quota → final boss Atiel]

Wind path → left for Gelkos → right for Nazmun → glide across → clear mobs. Stage 1–3: mobs in Nazmun room suffice. Stage 4–6: 130 mobs. Stage 7–10: 150 mobs.

---

## Deus — Final Boss: Atiel

- **Chain link:** Two players linked; DoT if close. Move apart to break.
- **Mark + bind:** One player bound. Stack in circle to split damage.
- **Terrain destruction:** Boss center → knockback waves. Edges collapse → fall = no res. Stay center.
- **Stagger (wipe):** ~20–30% HP. Deplete stagger in time or wipe. Pushback waves. Save stagger skills.

**Stage 4–6:** "Soul Knots" add; near = heavy slow + DoT. Avoid.

**Stage 4–6:** "사슬에 묶여라!" — all chained to Atiel. Stay close or take DoT. Different from "내게서 벗어날 수 없다!" chain.

**Stage 7–10:** "Judgment" stacks on hit. 3 stacks = floor spawns.

**Stage 7–10:** Chain links all 4. Move away from chain center to break.

---

## Shattered Arcanis — Route

30 mobs in plaza → 1st named False Siliaitor → 2nd named Ruthalis → final boss. Stage 4–6: 150 mobs. Stage 7–10: 170 mobs. Mobs are mostly ranged; watch aggro.

---

## Arcanis — 1st Named: False Siliaitor

**Guided saws:** 2 random targets, red line + saw. Cross lines (X) so saws collide.

**Boomerang:** Boss center → saws + boomerangs. Sidestep or jump on return.

---

## Arcanis — Final Boss: Void Talisra

- **Color orbs:** Assign 2 white, 2 black. Eat only your color. After, shields spawn; don't overlap.
- **Guided laser:** Big circle = target. Others stay back.
- **4 spears:** Break white spear (hit count, not damage). Fail = heavy AoE.
- **Jump rope:** Jump blue, then red. Red = stun.

**Stage 4–6:** "굴절의 창" — one player crystallized. Adjust paths so 3 spears hit crystal. Dodge spears before they fire.

**Stage 7–10:** Fire pillars; dodge through gaps. Can overlap with jump rope.
