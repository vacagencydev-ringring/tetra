## Pet Understanding (Analysis)

1. Each Analysis = XP equal to open slots (1★=100, 2★=200…)
2. **Transfer grade** at 4★, **Unique** at 7★, **Hero** at 9★
3. Lock options with padlock (costs more)
4. Slots 3 & 9: prefer **Power** or **Damage amp**
5. **Special** species: 6-slot only has defense; others attack-focused

### Tips
- Unique high roll ≈ Hero mid roll — locking Unique is fine
- Power (강타) is strong; aim for it on slots 3 & 9
- Special species: analyze first (high versatility)
