## Core strategy

**Main quest + regional quest together.** Don't tunnel-vision main quest only. Regional quests in Elysea/Demon World often unlock awakening gates and give key rewards. Pick up both when you enter a new area.

**Specialization slots:** The moment you hit a level that unlocks a new skill or specialization slot, open your skill page and assign it. Left-click + right-click combo is your bread-and-butter early damage.

---

## Quest flow by stage

**1–30:** Follow main storyline. Grab regional quests in Verteron (Elyos) or Altgard (Asmodian) as you pass through. Use tutorial rewards and starter gear.

**30–50:** Main quest will send you to new zones. Clear regional quests in each zone before moving on. Your first sealed dungeon (e.g. Daeva Biological Research Base) opens around here—do it when the quest points you there.

**50+:** Awakening progress becomes important. Check Journal missions; some gate next story steps. Odd Energy is limited—spend it on high-value instant completions rather than burning it on weak reward phases.

---

## Systems to keep moving

- **Field relics:** Grab them while you move. Don't do a separate relic grind session.
- **Noble belt / PvE amulet:** Upgrade when you have materials. These are long-term stat foundations.
- **Pet:** Level it as you level. Feed soul crystals when you have them.

---

## Odd Energy

Odd Energy recharges slowly. Use it for:
- Instant Completion Ticket: Daily Dungeon (when you've already cleared)
- High-reward content you've unlocked but don't have time to repeat

Avoid dumping it early on low-tier rewards.

---

## Session tips

- **Short session (20–30 min):** Main + nearest regional quests only.
- **Medium (45–60 min):** Add one dungeon run or one permanent-growth task (relics, amulet).
- **Long (90+ min):** Add party content or weekly mission cleanup.

---

## Common errors

- **Main quest only:** You'll hit awakening gates with no way to pass. Always check regional quests in the area.
- **Sitting on specialization unlocks:** Every breakpoint level → open skill page before the next pull.
- **Burning Odd Energy on weak phases:** Save major use for better reward tiers.
