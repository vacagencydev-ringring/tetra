## What this guide solves
- New players often lose time by doing side content in the wrong order.
- This guide gives a route that keeps EXP flow, awakening progress, and permanent growth synchronized.
- Goal: level fast without creating long-term progression debt.

---

## Core route philosophy

1) Main quest and regional quest should move together.
- Main quest alone eventually stalls at awakening progress gates.
- Regional quests provide key side rewards while you are already in the same area.

2) Sealed dungeons and outposts should be paced, not spammed blindly.
- Some rewards scale better when your progression is higher.
- Use travel-efficient timing instead of forcing full clear early.

3) Skill level and specialization slots are part of leveling speed.
- At each unlock breakpoint, assign specializations immediately.
- Early damage consistency usually comes from left-click + right-click core pairing.

---

## Permanent growth systems to never ignore

## Field relic collection
- Collect while moving, not as a separate marathon session.
- Relic progress fuels systems that provide persistent character value.

## PvE amulet progression
- Treat amulet as a long-term stat foundation.
- Upgrade cadence matters more than random burst spending.

## Noble belt progression
- Belt progression is a persistent power track.
- Route should be integrated into weekly movement, not isolated.

---

## Odd Energy timing
- Odd Energy is a strategic resource, not "spend everything now" fuel.
- Overusing it during weak reward phases lowers your total weekly value.
- Save major usage for stronger farm windows once your character is ready.

---

## 3-stage leveling framework

## Stage 1: Early progression
- Push main quest objectives.
- Clear overlapping regional quests.
- Stabilize first specialization setup.

## Stage 2: Mid progression
- Continue synchronized quest route.
- Add selective side systems that have clear permanent return.
- Maintain controlled Odd Energy usage.

## Stage 3: Pre-endgame transition
- Clean up delayed progression systems.
- Tune core skills for dungeon consistency.
- Prepare your first weekly repeatable routine.

---

## Common mistakes and fixes

Mistake: Main quest tunnel vision.
Fix: Always check if nearby regional quests unblock your next gate.

Mistake: Over-investing temporary gear.
Fix: Spend by replacement horizon (short hold vs long hold).

Mistake: Forgetting specialization unlocks.
Fix: Every breakpoint level -> open skill page before next pull.

Mistake: Burning Odd Energy too early.
Fix: reserve major consumption for better reward tiers.

---

## Session templates

30-minute session:
- Main + nearest regional quests only.

60-minute session:
- Main + regional + one permanent growth checkpoint.

90-minute session:
- Main + regional + one controlled farm block + reset prep.

Choose by schedule, but keep consistency.

---

## Quick checklist
- [ ] Main and regional quests are running in parallel.
- [ ] Newly unlocked specialization slots are assigned.
- [ ] Permanent growth systems are progressing weekly.
- [ ] Odd Energy is reserved for high-value windows.
- [ ] Gear spending is aligned with replacement timing.

---

## Practical conclusion
Fast leveling is not only speed; it is sequence quality. If you synchronize quest flow, specialization timing, and permanent growth systems, you level quickly and enter endgame without cleanup bottlenecks.
