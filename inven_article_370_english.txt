## 🔴 Don’t Forget to Check Daily! Essential Daily Tasks

### Mission Quests
- Available **5 times per day**
- Check rewards and use resets to aim for what you want
- **Wanted Quests** (수배령) have a chance for Unique-grade gear — don’t skip these
- Highly recommended as a must-do daily

### Supply Request (보급 의뢰)
- Types: Emergency (daily), Weekly, Season: Crafting, Season: Boss
- **Emergency** resets every day
- Turn in gear matching conditions to earn Abyss Points
- Abyss Point amount varies by material/equipment grade
- Optional in early game, but do when you can

### Black Cloud Trading Company (검은 구름 무역단)
- Refreshes hourly
- Buy Kinah equipment skins, pets, etc.
- Some pets are only available via Shugo Shop and Black Cloud Trading — prioritize these
- Pet bonus effects improve with comprehension; buy when you have spare Kinah
- Tradeable items can be bought, but market prices are high — approach with caution

## 📦 Spec Up First? Entrance Ticket / Daily Content to Stack

Content that charges entries or rewards daily, but can be done slowly depending on your situation. If not geared yet, tackling these after improving specs helps.

Still, if tickets stack too much, it gets tedious and you may lose value, so do them whenever you have time.

### Expedition Dungeon (원정 던전)
- Modes: Exploration, Conquest
- **Conquest** rewards charge 3 times daily (05:00, 13:00, 21:00)
- Can get Unique and Hero gear
- Need **Odd Energy** for guaranteed expedition rewards — check before entering
- Conquest: reward count shared across all dungeons
- Exploration: per-dungeon reward count (some dungeons currently have temporary limit removal)

### Transcendence (초월)
- 4-player upper raid; each dungeon has stages; S-rank unlocks next stage
- Suggested Combat Power: 1,200 (recommended: 1,400–1,500)
- Main rewards: Arcanas that help increase combat power
- Season rewards based on seasonal ranking

### Nightmare (악몽)
- Fight specific enemies for rewards; **5 tickets charge daily at 5:00**
- Exchange shop: Clash Rune Box, Devanion Crystal: Ariel, Wisdom Stone, etc.
- First-clear and repeat rewards
- Requires recommended combat power and has ranking; avoid ticket overflow

### Shugo Festa (슈고 페스타)
- Runs at **:15** and **:45** every hour
- Keys charge at **5:00 daily**; Membership doubles key charge and cube selection
- Simple minigame but solid **Unique gear drop chance**
- Exchange shop: Wisdom Stone, Devanion Crystals, etc.

### Dimension Invasion (차원 침공)
- Held at fixed times; many players clear summoned enemies
- Similar to Shugo Festa rewards (except Famous Painting Shards)

## 📅 Wednesday 5:00 AM Reset — Weekly Tasks

### Daily Dungeon (일일 던전)
- **7 times per week** (despite the name)
- Main source of enhancement stones
- Amount varies by final score — gear up and avoid letting tickets overflow

### Awakening Battle (각성전)
- Solo boss content: **7 → 3 times per week**
- Combat Power 1,000+ for Easy
- Drops: Sillentium, Advanced Awakening Inscription Box, Ariel’s Trace, etc.
- Ariel’s Trace can be converted to Ariel Crystals via Material Conversion

### Subjugation Battle (토벌전)
- 4-player party: **7 → 3 times per week**
- Combat Power 1,000+ for Easy
- Enhancement stones, Breakthrough Stone fragments, etc.

### Abyss
- Base **7 hours per week**; Membership: **14 hours**
- Combat Power 1,000+ required
- Can wait until last — do all other spec-up content before focusing here (except Draupnir Dungeon)

### Season Weekly Rewards
- Points from Abyss, Arena, Transcendence, Nightmare, Challenge, etc.
- Rewards at specific point thresholds
- **Stage 3+**: includes Revival Stone; **8,000 points**: also gives Odd Energy — aim for these

### Battlefield (전장)
- 8v8; rules per content; Elyos vs Asmodians
- Can take time, but **Victory reward = 2,000 Abyss Points**
- Victory rewards up to **10 wins per week**

### Sanctuary (성역)
- End-game; 8 players in 2 parties
- **2 challenge chances per week**; **1 reward per week**
- Currently hard to participate (attempts ongoing toward late December)

### Odd Energy Crafting
- Added in Nov 22 update
- **7 Odd Energy crafts per week** (total 280 Odd Energy)
- Requires Odd (from Gathering, Expedition, etc.)

### Supply Request (보급 의뢰)
- **Weekly** type resets once per week
- Same structure as daily — turn in gear for Abyss Points

### Order Shop — Verteron, Altgard (지령서 상점)
- **12 orders per week**
- Buy from main town Order Shop, complete via monster hunting
- Abyss Points, Ariel’s Trace Shards, Small Odd Energy, etc.

### Abyss Order Shop (어비스 지령서 상점)
- Separate from above; **5 per grade, 25 total**
- Mainly hunt monsters in Abyss areas
- Abyss Points and various rewards
- Check mission area when accepting — avoid missions near enemy faction

## ⚠️ Sunday Reset! Zephyr Breeze Trading Association Special Items

With Membership, **Zephyr Breeze Trading Association** sells items that reset weekly.  
In the **Special** category you can buy with Kinah:
- Revival Spirit Stone
- Odd Energy
- Abyss Rift Stone
- Daily Dungeon ticket (Bio Research Base)
- Soul Crystals
- etc.

⚠️ **Important:** Unlike Wednesday 5:00 resets, these special items reset at **Sunday midnight → Monday** (not Wednesday early morning).
