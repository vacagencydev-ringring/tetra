## Rule one: mechanics before logs

Most wipes start with one ignored mechanic. A short damage cut is faster than a full reset. Controlled phase progression beats random burst attempts.

---

## Pre-run setup

**Before entering:**
- Confirm role (tank/heal/DPS)
- Check defensive skill timeline
- Verify required CP and entry conditions
- Agree on revive and reset policy

**Early attempts:**
- Map telegraph signals
- Call movements clearly
- Prioritize survival over damage greed

**After each wipe:** Identify the exact failure trigger. Change one variable at a time.

---

## Tier 4–6 differences

At higher transcendence tiers:
- Extra traits add complexity
- Kill requirements per area are stricter
- Patterns are upgraded versions + new cooperative mechanics

Old low-tier habits cause wipes. Expect new patterns, not just more HP.

---

## Deus Research Base

- **Soul Knots (Stage 4–6):** Adds spawn near players. Stay apart or take heavy slow + DoT.
- **Chain patterns:** "사슬에 묶여라!" chains everyone to Atiel—stay close or take DoT. Different from "내게서 벗어날 수 없다!" (two-player chain).
- **Stagger:** ~20–30% HP. Deplete stagger in time or wipe. Save stagger skills.
- **Terrain destruction:** Boss center knocks back. Edges collapse. Stay center to avoid fall death.

Use clear callouts for dialogue-linked mechanics.

---

## Shattered Arcanis

- **False Siliaitor:** Guided saws target 2 players. Cross lines (X) so saws collide.
- **Void Talisra:** Color orbs—assign 2 white, 2 black. Eat only your color. Break white spear (hit count, not damage). Jump rope: jump blue, then red (red = stun).
- **Stage 4–6:** "굴절의 창"—one player crystallized. Adjust so 3 spears hit crystal. Dodge before they fire.
- **Stage 7–10:** Fire pillars; dodge through gaps. Can overlap with jump rope.

Pre-assign roles: lure, release, call, backup.

---

## Call vocabulary

Keep calls short:
- "Hold burst"
- "Move out"
- "Stack in"
- "Break now"
- "Cleanse ready"

No long explanations during active patterns.

---

## Recovery discipline

Not every pull is recoverable. If major cooldown sync is broken, reset early. If the pattern caller is unsure, pause damage and re-stabilize.

---

## Progression model

**Week 1:** Pattern recognition, call vocabulary.

**Week 2:** Movement cleanup, defensive timing.

**Week 3:** Burst optimization only in verified safe windows.

Skipping this order usually causes unstable runs.
