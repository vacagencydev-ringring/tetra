# Conquest Krao Cave & Draupnir Guide (First-timers)

*Original: https://www.inven.co.kr/board/aion2/6444/458*

---

## Why Skip Bosses? Expedition Party Types

**All bosses:** Better Odd Energy Cube rewards at the end.

**Final boss only:** Wings crafting materials, Hall of Gods statue achievements. Draupnir and Fire Temple give Hall items at 10 Conquest clears. Fire Temple has 32 related achievements for extra items.

---

## Krao Cave — Route

Go left first → clear boss → cross to right → second boss → return to center, kill **Krao Seal Guardian** → wall behind Obelisk breaks → path to final boss. One fall zone; watch your step.

## Krao Cave — Final Boss: Complete Verk

- **Swing:** Safe behind boss. Often followed by Whirlwind.
- **Whirlwind:** Red dust → move away fast. High multi-hit damage if hit.
- **Rage Wave:** Hide behind yellow rocks within 5 sec or instant death.
- **Stagger (wipe):** At 50% HP, stagger gauge unlocks. Deplete in ~9 sec or wipe. Save high-stagger Stigma skills.

---

## Draupnir — Route

Ignore trash. Activate **Abyss Gate Control** → jump into hole. Glide left to Boss 1 (don’t land near entrance). After Boss 2, use Obelisk or walk to wind-path device for final boss.

For speed runs (Bakarma only): drop straight toward wind-path device (~5 min clear).

---

## Draupnir — Final Boss: Transcended Bakarma

**Stagger pattern** at ~66% and ~33% HP. **[Wavering Wave]:** Jump like jump rope (one beat early). Second stagger has black tornadoes; avoid contact. After first stagger, boss dives → one wave; watch for it.
