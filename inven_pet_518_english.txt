## What is Pet Understanding Work?

Pets are obtained by defeating field monsters and collecting souls to create mounts. Though it seems like a simple mount feature, the real value of pets lies elsewhere: they provide **internal stat growth** that boosts character performance regardless of item level — the **final end-game stage**. That’s **Pet Understanding**.

Pet understanding work means repeatedly hunting monsters that drop souls to gain various stats. Collect 10 souls to register that monster as a pet; at 50, 150, 300, 500, etc., you get more stats. Crit, Power, Accuracy, Dexterity, etc. all directly affect real combat damage.

Raising pet level or using **Analysis** increases **Species Understanding** (종족 이해도), which unlocks stronger, universal stats like Crit, HP, PVE Attack. At item level 2,100~2,400+, these internal stats become meaningful.

### Pet System Summary
- 10 kills: Register field monster as a **pet**
- 10/50/150/300/500 kills: Pet understanding + main stats increase
- Species understanding unlocks PVE Attack, Crit, etc.
- **Soul Crystals** (영혼 결정): Drop from pets with max understanding (5★)
- Simple rule: Hunt **1,010** per pet

## Species Understanding — Major Stat Gains

Understanding is accumulated **by species**, not per pet. For example, farming **Cogni** species (e.g. Krall) raises both that pet’s understanding and **Cogni species understanding**.

Analysis gives random bonus stats, but you can **reroll** them. Soul Crystals come from achievements or hunting max-level pets; the drop matches the species (e.g. Cogni Soul Crystal from max Cogni pets).

Upper-tier stats unlock at species understanding **4, 7, 9**. Until then, don’t over-use Soul Crystals. Early useful stats: **Spirit**, **HP**, **Boss Attack**.

### Species Understanding Summary
- Pet understanding sums by species
- Upper stats appear at stages 4, 7, 9
- Soul Crystals from max-understanding pets
- Early priority: Spirit, HP, Boss Attack

## Start With Cogni Pets to Level 3! Pet Work Guide

Pet work is the **final internal stage**. If you still have easier growth (gear, level), prioritize that first. When doing pet work, combine with **Abyss Supply Request** or **Mission Quests** for efficiency. Areas that drop Spirit Stones, leather, etc. are ideal.

**Method:** Select a pet → go to its spawn → hunt **1,010** of that monster. Repeat for all 206 pets. After maxing a pet, do Analysis and move on. For species level 10, you’ll need extra hunts for more Soul Crystals.

**Recommended:** **Cogni** — first option is HP, second is Crit. Do easier field pets first; some appear in Conquest dungeons, Transcendence, or bosses.

Full mastery needs 200,000+ kills — a **long-term project**.
