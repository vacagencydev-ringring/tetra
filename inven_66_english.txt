## Guide scope
- This is a long-running PVE Sorcerer update guide with patch-adjusted recommendations.
- It includes pre-16 and post-16 routes, plus scaling direction for higher investment.
- Main focus: reliable rotation flow with strong fire/ice utility interactions.

---

## Core active skill package

High-priority base skills:
- Flame Arrow line
- Ice Chain line
- Frigid Wind line
- Flame Burst line
- Focused Invocation line

Supporting actives:
- Flame Barrage
- Freeze and Freeze Burst interaction
- Hellfire field pressure tools

Core idea:
- Build a stable trigger network before chasing luxury max-level routes.

---

## Lv16 transition strategy

After reaching key pre-16 breakpoints:
- Transition into optimized post-16 sequence.
- Strengthen skills that provide cooldown coupling and burst consistency.
- Keep Focused Invocation integrated into your damage windows.

This transition is where many Sorcerer players gain or lose overall uptime.

---

## Priority logic for upgrades

1. Secure baseline core actives to practical levels.
2. Upgrade skills that unlock important specialization behavior.
3. Add passives that support sustained casting and resource control.
4. Expand into specialized burst tools after rotation consistency is stable.

---

## Passive direction

Recommended passive set emphasizes:
- Casting stability
- Resource sustain
- Elemental damage consistency
- Practical survivability in movement-heavy bosses

When points are tight:
- Delay lower-impact passives first.
- Keep high-impact sustain/cycle passives online.

---

## Rotation philosophy

- Sorcerer PVE is not only burst; it is rhythm management.
- Keep your core trigger cycle active.
- Avoid overloading animation-heavy casts during unsafe movement windows.
- Re-enter damage loop immediately after mechanics.

---

## Practical execution tips

- Track cooldown sync between your major burst and setup buttons.
- Use field/zone skills where boss uptime is predictable.
- Preserve mobile-friendly casts for forced movement phases.
- Rebuild rhythm quickly after interruption.

---

## Common mistakes

- Forcing full burst in every phase regardless of mechanics.
- Leveling too many side skills before core breakpoints.
- Ignoring cast safety and losing uptime to avoidable hits.
- Overvaluing dummy damage over real boss uptime.

---

## Build maintenance checklist

- [ ] Did I complete the pre-16 baseline before greed upgrades?
- [ ] Are my post-16 specializations aligned with my current dungeon tier?
- [ ] Is my passive setup helping real cast consistency?
- [ ] Do I maintain damage rhythm after movement mechanics?
- [ ] Are my burst windows aligned with actual boss vulnerability?

---

## Practical conclusion
Sorcerer PVE scaling comes from disciplined cycle management. Patch-aware specialization choices, proper transition timing at Lv16+, and consistent real-fight uptime matter more than isolated burst screenshots.
