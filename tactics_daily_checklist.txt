## Goal

Build a repeatable weekly routine that avoids burnout and last-day panic. Consistent progression beats irregular high-effort sessions.

---

## Daily order

1. Check Journal and mission board for high-value tasks.
2. Review energy and ticket leakage risk.
3. Do short mobile-friendly blocks first.
4. Move party-dependent tasks into prime-time.

---

## Weekly structure

**Early week:** Lock core weekly rewards (missions, Abyss, Shugo Festa if active).

**Midweek:** Party dungeons, high-risk content.

**Weekend:** Burn tickets, finish delayed missions, resource consumption.

Don’t pile everything near reset.

---

## Odd Energy

Budget Odd Energy weekly. Estimate inflow, define target consumption windows, avoid one-day overspend. Random gains are bonus, not baseline.

---

## Session length

**20–30 min:** Mission checks, short guaranteed rewards.

**45–60 min:** Daily core loop + one progression side task.

**90+ min:** Include party content or backlog cleanup.

---

## Odd Energy and tickets

- Track consumables and weekly-limited resources.
- Odd Energy should be reserved for high-value content.
- Use Instant Completion Ticket for Daily Dungeon only after you’ve cleared.

---

## Anti-chaos checklist

- [ ] Daily must-do tasks completed first
- [ ] Party content scheduled in active hours
- [ ] Weekly-limited tasks spread across days
- [ ] Odd Energy and ticket consumption monitored
- [ ] Sunday pre-reset audit (unresolved rewards, next-week priority)

---

## Simple template

**Per day:** 3 daily fixed tasks + 2 weekly accumulation tasks + resource log.

**Sunday review:** Unresolved weekly rewards, next-week priority list.

---

## Common failures

- Doing easy tasks only → lock at least one high-impact task per session.
- Ignoring resource logs → update a minimal tracker daily.
- Pushing hard content to reset day → move one hard block to midweek.
- Never adjusting → review and tweak after each reset.
