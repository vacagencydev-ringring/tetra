## Author intent and scope
- This is not a single absolute answer build.
- It is a practical PVE route tested in real dungeon rotations.
- Core focus: stable sustain, cooldown flow, stagger contribution, and clean cancel timing.

---

## Core active skills (recommended progression)

## 1) Crushing Wave
- Priority: very high, target Lv20.
- Lv8 specialization: +15% damage and life steal.
- Lv12 specialization: +crit hit cooldown reset.
- Lv20 specialization: +pull effect.
- Why pull in PVE: helps gather scattered mobs and reduces dead time between packs.

## 2) Sharp Strike
- Priority: high, at least Lv12.
- Key value: reduces Destructive Blow cooldown and improves multihit consistency.
- Also contributes to sustain through life-steal based setup.

## 3) Destructive Blow
- Priority: high, Lv16~20.
- Lv12: +20% skill speed, +combat-ready uptime.
- Lv20: adds multihit impact and improves burst windows.
- Practical use: no longer just a gap closer at higher skill mastery; used as buff+damage core.

## 4) Smash Down
- Priority: medium-high, Lv16~20.
- Strong value from no-recast style interaction and anti-immune trigger conditions.

## 5) Blade Rampage
- Priority: high in boss cycles, Lv16~20.
- Lv16 setup already gives strong all-skill cooldown pressure.
- Lv20 adds sustain layer for safer prolonged uptime.

## 6) Additional utility picks
- Ankle Slash (range/chain comfort)
- Dash Strike (distance + cooldown comfort)
- Situational picks depending on dungeon mechanics

---

## Passive priority
1. Combat Preparation (max)
2. Weak Point Analysis (max)
3. Veteran Counter (max)
4. Killing Intent/stack-based extra proc passive
5. Defensive posture and remaining passives by content need

---

## Stigma recommendations
- Zikel's Blessing: major stagger contribution in timed stagger checks.
- Assault Stance: speed and reset value.
- Grit: emergency survival / mechanic bypass utility.
- Perfect Guard: high value in pattern-heavy fights.

---

## Rotation examples

## Solo boss opener
Destructive Blow -> Zikel's Blessing -> Rage Wave -> sustain/damage fillers -> Blade Rampage during stagger -> Sharp Strike + Crushing Wave loop.

## Field mob farming
Crushing Wave + Sharp Strike loop, finish remaining targets with short-cooldown fillers.

## 4-man dungeon bosses
- Maintain sustained DPS loop rather than only burst.
- Use Blade Rampage on stagger windows.
- Promote movement-friendly skills for uptime during boss mechanics.

---

## Animation cancel (core execution)
- Disable skill queue when practicing manual cancel timing.
- Hold left-click core attack and weave right-click/key skills to cancel end frames.
- Goal: keep GCD flow tight while preserving survivability and resources.

---

## Practical conclusion
This build is optimized for real PVE stability: frequent cooldown cycling, good sustain, reliable stagger pressure, and controllable DPS through manual canceling.
