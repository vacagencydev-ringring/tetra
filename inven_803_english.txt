# Conquest Savage Horn Cave Guide — Know the Final Boss Mechanics!

*Original: https://www.inven.co.kr/board/aion2/6444/803*

---

## Route

No trash. Run past → bomb wall → **Boss 1: Cuhafu**. Climb → break left wall → drop into cave → **Boss 2: Shining Lycan Totem**. Break totem → door opens → **Boss 3: Necromancer Duan Ka**. After Duan Ka, ride tornado to final boss.

---

## Boss 1: Cuhafu

**"Krau Tushu!"** → boss center → floors + lightning orbs. Everyone eats orbs (mobility slows). Second howl → purple ring widens. Leftover orbs touching ring = extra explosions.

---

## Boss 2: Shining Lycan Totem

Gimmick boss. Totem spawns adds + bomb boxes at both ends. Can’t break totem until adds dead. DPS at ends for bombs; tank gathers adds in center; healer sustains.

---

## Boss 3: Necromancer Duan Ka

**Wand spin:** Floor explosions under each player. Don’t overlap or die.

**Lycan Summon:** Green orbs appear; only orb-eater can damage spirits. Kill within 15 sec.

**Soul Call:** Stagger locked. All pulled center. Lightning orbs close in; touch = stun. Run out before big explosion (~8 sec).

---

## Final Boss: Savage Horn Nuakum

- **Debuff:** Stacks from hits → floor spawns. Place at edge or overlap.
- **Claw combo:** Front cone — deadly. Safe behind boss; DPS from rear.
- **Gut grab:** Hand forward → pull. Stun → claw combo. Dodge back after stun break.
- **Clone:** "사냥감은 정해졌다!" — one player immobilized (green HP). Non-marked players eat clone hits. Then boss drops: big circle = one person tank; small = stack.
- **Blue Light of Destruction (~30% HP):** Central energy. Green HP target gets red orb. Hit energy 3x or wipe.
- **Red orb x2:** Similar to clone; share hits.
