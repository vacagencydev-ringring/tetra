# Conquest Urugugu Gorge & Barklon's Sky Island Guide

*Original: https://www.inven.co.kr/board/aion2/6444/521*

---

## Urugugu Gorge — Route

Forward → upper boss → back to lower boss → forward. Kill **Urugugu Wind Shaman** at end for wind path. Ride tornadoes to final boss. No trash needed; only final Obelisk required.

---

## Urugugu — Mid Boss: Captain Laul

When Laul says **"힘을 되돌려주겠다니!"** and pauses — **counter pattern**. Stop attacking or you reflect damage. Then move out for wide AoE.

---

## Urugugu — Final Boss: Sacred Auldeur

- **Enrage:** Yellow gauge under HP. Fail to kill in time = stronger boss.
- **Storm Strike:** Boss disappears; red marker on one player. Everyone stack to split damage.
- **Feather Grab:** Feathers → one player lifted and dropped. Press Space to break free.

---

## Barklon's Sky Island — Route

Debuff stacks over time; 3 stacks = bind. **Mist Spirit Stones** clear it (rare in boss fights). Run straight → first boss → fly down → break wall → wind path → **Odd Rock Spirit** for barrier. Jump at ledge (or use right tornado). At end, collect 4 elements and place on altar for final boss.

---

## Barklon — Mid Boss: Tiae

Don’t stand close — party proximity causes heavy DoT. **"활짝 피어라!"** → jump → burst flowers, then all stack tight. **Clock pattern:** dodge left, place poison tiles tightly. After 4 shots, jump slam — getting hit = knockback into poison = death.

---

## Barklon — Mid Boss: Tamon

Ground spike = petrify. One petrified = boss uses raid AoE. Red body = physical absorb; purple = magic absorb. Stop DPS when wrong color.

---

## Barklon — Final Boss: Barklon

- **Enrage:** Must kill before enrage or unwinnable.
- **Bind:** One target; green HP bar = pre-cast. Others run in and break bind.
- **Rock Prison:** Stagger locked; all pulled in. Destroy red rock to escape. Don’t waste Stigma during lock.
- **Rising Spikes:** Red floor → jump for each wave. After 3rd wave, jump again or get AoE bind.
