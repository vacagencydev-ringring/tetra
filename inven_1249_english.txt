# Dead Dramata's Nest Guide

*Original: https://www.inven.co.kr/board/aion2/6444/1249*

---

## Route

Simple path but two underwater sections + one poison floor section.

**Underwater:** White orbs = speed; yellow = breath. Share orbs with party. After water, stick to left wall to avoid adds.

**Second boss path:** Large mushroom area; gliding can help skip adds.

**Poison floors:** After second boss, Dramata appears. First poison — thread between floors; second — run right edge.

**Final path:** Another underwater section. Avoid glowing mist (damage + knockback).

---

## Mid Boss: Hatching Nayatman

**Lightning wisps:** Slow but many. Dodge before explosion.

**Red blood, blue shell:** Two players linked. Distance = DoT. Move apart to break link → red/blue shields. Re-stack to remove shields or get stunned.

**Spear + explosion:** Spear marks → expanding explosion. Sometimes multiple.

---

## Mid Boss: Administrator Najium

Save stamina for **Guided Blades**. "막을 수 없는 칼날이 끝까지 널 분쇄하리라" — run behind stone pillars to break blades (similar to Arcanis 1st named).

---

## Final Boss: Corrupt Durvati

- **Poison rain:** "독의 비를 뒤집어쓰거라!" — mark per player. Rain falls 4x; aim at small/big eggs. Small = 1 hit, big = 3 hits. Break small first.
- **Roar:** After poison rain. Not close = fear and DPS loss. Stick close after placing poison.
- **Venomous swamp:** "독에 물든 대지가 너희의 무덤이 되리라" — 4 sec timer. Climb rocks 2+ levels high to avoid breath.
- **Add spawn (~30% HP):** "깨어나라, 나의 아이들아!" — eggs hatch. Small eggs = mobs that break rocks; big = attack players. Kill small-egg spawns first.

---

## Hard Mode Differences

**Venomous swamp:** One player per rock only (debuff). Last swamp = "Rising Venomous Swamp" — climb higher.
