# Kaisinel — EP6 Final Story Boss Guide

*Original: https://www.inven.co.kr/board/aion2/6444/249*

---

## Overview

Kaisinel is the EP6 story final boss — a level 45 gatekeeper. Expect many AoE floors and adds. Save dodge (Shift) for the three main dangerous patterns; for others, walk out or take hits. Bring buff consumables and use them liberally.

---

## Tips

- Use buff items: Enhancement Scrolls, Soul Stones, Potions, Food
- Use dodge only on the three main dangerous patterns
- Statue-explosion pattern: walk out — no dodge needed
- When adds spawn in late phase: create distance to make Kaisinel blink to you and avoid getting sandwiched

---

## Main Patterns (Dangerous Only)

**1. Orb Throw** — Most painful normal attack; hard to read. If orb appears above boss, dodge.

**2. Yellow Floor x5** — Common pattern; forces two dodges. When staff points at you, roll quickly.

**3. Blink + Butterfly** — High damage + stun. When boss blinks, stick close immediately to avoid this.

**4. Statue Spawn** — Safe spots at first; later, no gaps. Walk out slowly.

**5. Add Summon** — Fire → Water → Earth, then Dark Spirit (persists and attacks). Kill the Dark Spirit or ignore and focus boss. Tip: run out so Kaisinel blinks to you; adds walk slowly.
