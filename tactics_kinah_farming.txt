## Multi-character farming

Players running 6 characters with a strict routine report **~100M+ kinah per week** in field + dungeon farming. This depends on class, gear, queue luck, and market timing—not guaranteed.

**Key idea:** Kinah loss usually comes from poor scheduling, not lack of playtime.

---

## Energy budget

Each character has limited energy (Odd Energy + shop/crafting sources). Plan runs like this:

1. Calculate per-character weekly energy.
2. Convert energy → run count (based on cost per run).
3. Multiply by roster size.
4. Add buffer for failed runs and inefficiency.

If you skip the math, your week collapses near reset.

## Time-slot tips

**Mobile-friendly:** Short tasks, mission setup, exchange checks, quick purchases.

**PC prime-time:** Party dungeons (Deus Research Base, Shattered Arcanis, Ludra), Abyss runs, Shugo Festa.

**Low-traffic hours:** Solo content, daily missions, material farming.

**Midweek:** Push high-yield party content while queues are active.

**Weekend:** Burn tickets, finish delayed tasks.

---

## Alt roles

Don't run every alt the same way.

- **Energy burners:** Characters that eat energy on high-value dungeons.
- **Routine handlers:** Daily mission completion, exchange maintenance.
- **Utility alts:** Specific resource loops (e.g. gathering, material conversion).
- **AP/Abyss alts:** Optional PvP-focused farming.

---

## Selling drops

- **Sell immediately:** High-volume, low-volatility items.
- **Time your sell:** Market-sensitive drops (e.g. rare materials after patch).

Track exchange trends instead of dumping everything on day one.

---

## Mistakes to avoid

- Doing a full clear routine every day with no role split → burnout, lower yield.
- Ignoring reset windows → missed weekly rewards.
- Over-investing in alts → main character falls behind.
- No solo backup when queues are dead → wasted sessions.
